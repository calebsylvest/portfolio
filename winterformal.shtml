Back in December the Wesley Foundation at Louisiana Tech had their annual winter formal dance. The theme was to be based on the 1920s feel, with the underground parties and such, (think Great Gatsby). I branded the dance as The Roaring Twenties Winter Formal and created this poster as advertisement. Also as a sidenote, I won a Silver Addy Award for the design.

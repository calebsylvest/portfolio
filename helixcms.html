Helix CMS

  • Client: We Are Interstellar
  • Role: Co-Creator, Copy Writing, Design, Development
  • URL: www.HelixPowered.com

Helix CMS was devised out of frustration - frustration at the overwhelming complexity of building sites using platforms like Wordpress and Drupal. The idea for Helix hatched while talking with my friend and business partner, Joshua Hodges, each admitting to a distaste for the bloated systems and seeking a solution.

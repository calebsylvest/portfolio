In my Adanced Multimedia class we were given the task of developing a touch screen kiosk to be used in Paris, France. I decided I would develop a touch screen drawing app for children of famous structures in Paris. At the top is a simple nav system allowing the user to change the main image. At the bottom is a simple drawing tool, with a pencil, eraser, text, and color options. The app would be connected to the Internet allowing the user to "save" the image by emailing it to themselves or emailing to family members they may want to share it with.

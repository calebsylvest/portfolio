Rebirth Screenprint

The Rebirth poster is one of my favorite pieces I have ever made. Imagery comes from the graphic novel series Phoenix by the legendary Osamu Tezuka.

The image is screen printed on thick chipboard using five colors - cyan, magenta, yellow, black, white, & gold. Five screens were employed for printing; in this order: 1. Black cloud background 2. Gold stars and starburst 3. White base 4. Cyan, Magenta, & Yellow for elements 5. Black over-layer.
